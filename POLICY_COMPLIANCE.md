# AdSense Policy Compliance Checklist

## Pre-Approval Requirements

Before submitting your site to Google AdSense, ensure all of the following are in place:

### Content Requirements
- [x] **Original Content**: All articles must be original, not copied from other sources
- [x] **Substantial Content**: At least 15-20 quality articles (aim for 1000+ words each)
- [x] **No Thin Content**: Pages have meaningful text, not just images or videos
- [x] **Regular Updates**: Content should be fresh and regularly updated
- [x] **No Prohibited Topics**: 
  - No adult/sexual content
  - No gambling or casino content
  - No drugs, tobacco, or weapons
  - No hacking, cracking, or warez
  - No violent or graphic content
  - No hate speech or discrimination

### Technical Requirements
- [x] **Clear Navigation**: Users can easily find all pages
- [x] **Working Links**: No broken links or 404 errors
- [x] **Fast Loading**: Page load times under 3 seconds
- [x] **Mobile Responsive**: Works perfectly on mobile devices
- [x] **No Malware**: Site is clean and secure
- [x] **Valid HTML**: No serious markup errors
- [x] **Sitemap**: XML sitemap submitted to Google Search Console
- [x] **Robots.txt**: Properly configured

### Legal Pages (Required by AdSense)
- [x] **About Page**: Shows who runs the site, establishes authority
- [x] **Privacy Policy**: 
  - Explains cookie use
  - Mentions Google AdSense specifically
  - Includes DART cookie disclosure
  - Links to Google Privacy Policy
  - Opt-out instructions for personalized ads
- [x] **Terms of Service**: 
  - User conduct rules
  - Disclaimer of warranties
  - Limitation of liability
  - Third-party advertising disclosure

### Design & User Experience
- [x] **No Excessive Ads**: Currently no ads (applying for first time)
- [x] **Content Above Fold**: Main content visible without scrolling
- [x] **No Pop-ups**: No intrusive interstitials or pop-ups
- [x] **Professional Design**: Clean, organized, easy to read
- [x] **Clear Content Structure**: Headings, paragraphs, proper formatting

### Traffic & Engagement (Post-Approval)
- [ ] **Organic Traffic**: Focus on SEO and organic discovery
- [ ] **No Paid Traffic**: Don't buy traffic or use traffic exchanges
- [ ] **No Incentivized Clicks**: Never ask users to click ads
- [ ] **No Auto-Refresh**: Don't auto-reload pages for impressions
- [ ] **No Click Fraud**: Never click your own ads

## Content Guidelines for Tech Blog

### Acceptable Content Topics ✅
- Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
- Cloud Computing and DevOps
- Web Development tutorials
- Software Engineering best practices
- Programming language comparisons
- Technology news and analysis
- Career advice for developers
- Tool and framework reviews

### Content to Avoid ❌
- Pirated software or tools
- Hacking tutorials (malicious)
- Circumventing security measures
- Encouraging policy violations
- Excessive profanity
- Personal attacks or harassment

## E-E-A-T Requirements (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trust)

### Your Site Already Has:
1. **About Page**: Establishes who is behind the content
2. **Author Information**: Blog posts include author details in schema
3. **Privacy Policy**: Shows transparency about data use
4. **Contact Information**: Email for contact
5. **Structured Data**: Article schema with author, publisher info

### To Improve Further:
- [ ] Add author bios to individual blog posts
- [ ] Include credentials/experience in About page
- [ ] Add social media profiles (LinkedIn, Twitter)
- [ ] Get backlinks from other tech sites
- [ ] Regular posting schedule

## Post-Approval Maintenance

### Weekly Checks
- [ ] Monitor AdSense dashboard for policy violations
- [ ] Check for invalid traffic alerts
- [ ] Review ad performance and placement

### Monthly Checks
- [ ] Update Privacy Policy if practices change
- [ ] Review and update outdated content
- [ ] Check for broken links
- [ ] Monitor Core Web Vitals in Search Console

### Never Do (Will Get Banned)
- ❌ Click your own ads
- ❌ Ask others to click ads
- ❌ Use bots or automated tools on ads
- ❌ Place ads on thank you, login, or error pages
- ❌ Modify AdSense code
- ❌ Show ads on pages with no content
- ❌ Encourage accidental clicks (ads near buttons)
- ❌ Place more than 3 ad units per page

## GDPR & Cookie Compliance

### Implemented ✅
- Cookie consent banner on first visit
- Granular consent options (Essential, Analytics, Advertising)
- No advertising cookies until consent given
- Link to Google Ad Settings
- Clear privacy policy explanation
- Easy opt-out mechanism

### Required Disclosures in Privacy Policy:
- Third-party vendors (Google AdSense) ✓
- Cookie usage explanation ✓
- DART cookie mention ✓
- Opt-out instructions ✓
- Data usage explanation ✓
